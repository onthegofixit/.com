Tips for caring for your phone

Keep in mind these recommendations to take care of your cellphone and extend its lifespan.

Charging your battery

We recommend charging your cellphone when the battery reaches 10%, connect it to power until it reaches 100% without disconnecting it, as this will extend the battery's lifespan.

Update the operating system of your device.

Take advantage when the manufacturer releases a new version of the operating system and update it to improve upon the previous version, resulting in more optimal performance.

Exposure to water

Avoid getting your cellphone wet and exposing it to humid environments, as this can cause internal damage to your smartphone. If your model is water-resistant, check the manufacturer's specifications for the conditions it can withstand.
